# Product Mission: Flor Guzman Portfolio

## Product Vision

Create a stunning, minimal, and modern portfolio website that showcases Flor Guzman's work as a photographer and art director, serving as a powerful digital business card to attract potential clients and partners.

## Core Purpose

The website exists to:
- **Showcase Excellence**: Display Flor Guzman's photography, video work, and sketchup drawings in an elegant, distraction-free environment
- **Build Connections**: Make it easy for potential clients and partners to discover and connect with Flor
- **Communicate Value**: Clearly present Flor's capabilities and artistic vision to drive business opportunities
- **Provide Accessibility**: Serve both English and Spanish-speaking audiences through bilingual support

## Target Audience

### Primary Users
- **Potential Clients**: Individuals and businesses seeking professional photography and art direction services
- **Creative Partners**: Other professionals in the creative industry looking for collaboration opportunities
- **Art Enthusiasts**: People interested in viewing and appreciating high-quality photography and artistic work

### User Needs
- Quick access to portfolio samples across different categories
- Easy navigation through work categories (photo sessions, shoots, sketchup drawings)
- Ability to learn about Flor's background and approach
- Seamless experience in their preferred language (English/Spanish)

## Product Differentiators

1. **Bilingual Experience**: Native support for English and Spanish speakers in the creative industry
2. **Category-Focused Navigation**: Sticky footer with quick anchors to specific work categories
3. **Multi-Media Showcase**: Integrates both static images and video content seamlessly
4. **Minimal, Modern Design**: Clean aesthetic that puts the work front and center without distraction
5. **Performance-First**: Static site architecture ensures fast loading and optimal user experience

## Success Metrics

- Portfolio content is easily accessible and navigable
- Users can view work in their preferred language
- Site loads quickly and performs smoothly across devices
- Content can be easily updated and deployed
- Professional presentation that reflects the quality of the work

## Strategic Approach

This is a content-focused, single-page static website that prioritizes:
- **Visual Impact**: High-quality media presentation
- **User Experience**: Intuitive navigation and smooth interactions
- **Maintainability**: Simple content updates through modern deployment workflows
- **Accessibility**: Bilingual support and responsive design
- **Reliability**: Automated testing and CI/CD for consistent quality
